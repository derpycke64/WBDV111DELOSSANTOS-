FAQ

What’s the difference between Black Card and Classic memberships?
With a Black Card, you will have access to all Rye Fitness gyms available, while the Classic membership gives you access to your local gym or where it was bought. Additionally, as a Black Card member you will have access to several amenities that are not available to Classic members such as the option to bring a guest anytime, and other exclusive Black Card perks.
How do I make changes to my membership?
You can make changes to your membership by contacting your local branch or logging into your account online at My Account | Rye Fitness .
How old do I have to be to get a Rye Fitness membership?
You can join RF if you are 13 years or older with a parent/legal guardian's permission. Members aged 13 must be accompanied by a parent or guardian when they work out. Many clubs also require that members aged 14 must be accompanied by a parent/guardian at all times. Members who are 15-17 years old (15-18 in regions where 19 is the age of majority) must have a signed waiver from a parent or guardian. For further questions or clarifications regarding age policy, reach out to your local branch for more detail
