Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my membership anytime?

Yes! All memberships are month-to-month with no long-term contract. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice.

Do you offer student discounts?

Absolutely! Students receive 20% off all membership plans with valid student ID.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year for medical or travel reasons.

What's included in personal training?

Each session includes customized workout plans, form correction, and progress tracking with a certified trainer.
