Our History and Founder

Rye Fitness was founded in 2021 by Rey Canlas, a former bodybuilding competitor with a passion for fitness and a goal of bringing quality training to the province of Santa Catalina, Negros Occidental.

Having competed in the sport himself, Rey understood what it took to build a strong, disciplined body, and more importantly, he wanted to share that knowledge with his community. What started as a small local gym has grown into a welcoming space for people of all fitness levels, from complete beginners to serious athletes looking to push their limits.
